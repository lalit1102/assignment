Audio Tag with Properties

Audio element with these properties:

  • controls: Shows playback controls
  • muted: Audio is initially muted
  • loop: Audio will loop continuously
  • autoplay: Audio will play automatically when page loads

Video Tag with Properties

Video element with these properties:

  • controls: Shows playback controls
  • muted: Video is initially muted
  • loop: Video will loop continuously
  • autoplay: Video will play automatically when page loads
